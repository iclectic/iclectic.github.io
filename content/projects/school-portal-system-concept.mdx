## Overview

A full stack concept for a digital school portal that supports communication between students, teachers, and parents.

## Problem

Schools often struggle with fragmented systems for communication, results, and assignments.

## Solution

A centralised portal where students, teachers, parents, and administrators can manage academic information and communication in one place.

## Key features

- Student dashboards
- Teacher result uploads
- Parent access portals
- Assignment sharing
- Messaging between parents and teachers

## Planned stack

- Modern frontend framework
- Backend APIs with secure authentication
- Scalable data storage
## Outcome

A product concept that brings structure, clarity, and accountability to school communication workflows.
